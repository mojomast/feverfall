## Rating Questions

Rate each from 1 strongly disagree to 5 strongly agree.

- I understood where the first bounce would go before firing.
- The physics felt fair, even when later bounces surprised me.
- The ball felt too floaty.
- The ball felt too pinball-like or chaotic.
- Bucket catches felt skillful and satisfying.
- Peg hits were readable.
- Near misses were understandable.
- Board clear payoff was exciting without becoming annoying.
- I wanted to take one more shot after the test ended.

## Comprehension Check

- What do orange pegs mean?
- What does the bucket do?
- What helped you aim?
- Which collision or bounce felt least fair?
